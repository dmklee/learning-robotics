High School Pilot Program

Spring 2021

We partnered with the Center for STEM Education at Northeastern University to run a six-week outreach program for local high school students. Due to constraints from COVID, we operated virtually; each student received their own robotic arm and we provided weekly lectures and office hours over video call. Projects included: sequencing arm movements, face tracking with visual servoing, and stacking cubes. If you are interested in running a similar program, please reach out to me and I would be happy to talk about it or share curriculum plans.
