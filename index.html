Our Goal

We aim to expand access to robotics. We believe that hands-on experience is key to cultivating excitement in robotics and STEM. This robotic kit, at only $250, is a great educational platform for students ranging from high school to college level.

Robot Kit

At under $300, the kit includes a 5-DoF arm with accurate servos and realtime position feedback, and a 1MP camera to locate objects in the workspace.

Simulator

The robot kit can be simulated on Windows, Mac and Linux, allowing for virtual teaching experiences at no cost.
